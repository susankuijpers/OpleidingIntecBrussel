About


Hi i'm Termagant and i'm a graphic designer, illustrator and UI designer. Studio Termagant is a Antwerp based graphic design studio founded by Susan Kuijpers. She graduated printmaking at Sint Lucas School of arts, Antwerp in 2016. Studio. Termagant currently operates in the field of webdesign, identity design, print design. Over the years Termagant has worked with network of skilled designers/artists like InTheEndBooks?, Patrice Lesuer, Nanoe Carremans, etc. Follow Studio Termagant on Instagram for further insights & updates. Or you can mail me for work or collaborations.
