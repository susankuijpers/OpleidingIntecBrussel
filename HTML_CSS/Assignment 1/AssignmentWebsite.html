Patrice Lesueur

            Patrice Lesueur has an extensive career within skateboarding. 
            he officially turned pro in 2010 with New Deal Skateboards, he has influenced many through his skating, 
            photography,art and nature, and played a big role in the Antwerp skate scene. 
            
            Lesueur’s earlier photography is possibly the most influential of his work. 
            He would shoot fellow team members and pros on tour and at home, giving the viewer an 
            inside look into the lifestyle of him and his friends that was otherwise unseen, and often pushing beyond 
            skateboarding to document other similar groups of people and alternative lifestyles and nature.
